About

TerHandle is a groundbreaking concept that connects technicians with consumers facing issues with electronic devices at home. With TerHandle, consumers no longer need to leave their houses to seek service centers. Simply schedule a service appointment, and a technician will come to you.

Features

  • Login & Register
  • Register as a technician
  • Request service
  • View service status
  • View technician profile
  • View technician location
  • View technician rating
  • View technician review
  • View technician service schedule
